Easily looking into a squashfs
==============================

You want to do something inside a squashfs, as a user?

    $ guestfish --ro -a ovirt-node-ng-image.squashfs.img run : mount /dev/sda / : mount-loop /LiveOS/rootfs.img / : sh "cat /etc/os-release"
